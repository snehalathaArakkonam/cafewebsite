Our Story

Founded in 2024, CAFE & REFRESH started with a simple vision: to create a space where coffee lovers could enjoy premium brews in a setting that feels like home, yet exudes elegance.

Located in the beautiful city of Tirupati, we take pride in our heritage and our commitment to quality. Every bean is selected with care, and every dish is prepared with passion.

Whether you're here for your morning caffeine fix, a business meeting, or a cozy date, we strive to make every visit a refreshing experience.

Our Core Values

Quality

We never compromise on the quality of our ingredients or our service.

Community

We believe in building a strong community around our shared love for coffee.

Innovation

We constantly explore new flavors and techniques to surprise your palate.
